```bash

bandit28@bandit:/tmp/myrepo2/repo$gitlog
...
bandit28@bandit:/tmp/myrepo2/repo$gitshow
...
bandit28@bandit:/tmp/myrepo2/repo$echo"d1VDeCFsL3N1MXV6MFJzT1o1ajFHd1V2Z1E="|rev|base64-d
```
